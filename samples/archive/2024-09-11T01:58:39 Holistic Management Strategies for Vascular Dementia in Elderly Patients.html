Modifiable risk factors, such as high blood pressure, diabetes, obesity, smoking, and a sedentary lifestyle, are commonly linked with both stroke and vascular dementia. By addressing these factors through lifestyle changes, such as regular physical activity, dietary improvements, and smoking cessation, individuals can significantly reduce their risk of stroke and, consequently, dementia.
